Privacy Policy for Eisenhower ToDo Management

Effective Date: 5 June 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Eisenhower ToDo Management! This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application. By accessing or using our application, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

Cookies and Application Data: Eisenhower ToDo Management uses cookies and stores your username, your (encrypted) password, your ToDo lists and items in a database.

Other Data: We do not collect any other data from you. However, the application is hosted by Fly.io and their privacy policy applies to their service.

How We Use Cookies

Cookie Information: We use cookies to keep you logged into your account.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties. However, please note that our application may link to external websites or services. Their privacy policies govern the use of your data on those platforms.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure. Please use our application responsibly and avoid sharing sensitive information.

Your Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some features of our application may not function correctly. At any point, you may export your ToDo lists and items using the link in the header menu. Deleting your account removes all data and is irreversible.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Please check this page for the latest version.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at jaerrib@tutanota.com.
